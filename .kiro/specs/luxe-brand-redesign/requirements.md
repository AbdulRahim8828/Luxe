# Requirements Document

## Introduction

Complete rebranding and redesign of the furniture polishing website from "A1 Furniture Polish" to "Luxe Wooden Furniture Polishing" with a luxury positioning, premium design aesthetic, and comprehensive content updates while maintaining SEO performance.

## Glossary

- **Brand_System**: The complete visual identity including colors, typography, and design elements
- **Content_Management_System**: The system managing all textual content across the website
- **SEO_Manager**: The system handling search engine optimization and URL structures
- **Asset_Manager**: The system managing images, icons, and media files
- **Navigation_System**: The website's menu and routing structure
- **Component_Library**: Reusable UI components throughout the website

## Requirements

### Requirement 1: Brand Identity Transformation

**User Story:** As a business owner, I want to completely rebrand from "A1 Furniture Polish" to "Luxe Wooden Furniture Polishing", so that the website reflects our new luxury positioning and premium service offering.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN the website loads, THE Brand_System SHALL display "Luxe Wooden Furniture Polishing" as the primary brand name
2. WHEN users view any page, THE Brand_System SHALL use the luxury color palette (Jet Black #0E0E0E, Royal Gold #C9A24D, Champagne Gold #E6D3A3, Ivory White #F5F5F5, Warm Grey #9A9A9A)
3. WHEN displaying headings, THE Brand_System SHALL use Playfair Display serif font with subtle letter spacing
4. WHEN displaying body text, THE Brand_System SHALL use Poppins font for clean readability
5. WHEN showing the hero section, THE Brand_System SHALL capitalize "LUXE" and display the tagline "Luxury Finish for Timeless Furniture"

### Requirement 2: Visual Design System Implementation

**User Story:** As a potential customer, I want the website to convey luxury and professionalism through its visual design, so that I trust the service quality and feel confident booking premium furniture care.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Component_Library SHALL implement a minimal luxury design style with generous white space
2. WHEN users interact with elements, THE Component_Library SHALL provide subtle hover animations
3. WHEN displaying content, THE Component_Library SHALL use high contrast between text and backgrounds
4. WHEN highlighting important elements, THE Component_Library SHALL use gold accents sparingly and strategically
5. WHEN users view the site on mobile devices, THE Component_Library SHALL maintain the luxury aesthetic with mobile-first responsive design

### Requirement 3: Content Strategy and Messaging

**User Story:** As a content manager, I want all website content to reflect the luxury positioning and professional tone, so that the messaging aligns with the premium brand identity.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN users read any content, THE Content_Management_System SHALL use professional, confident, and premium tone
2. WHEN displaying service descriptions, THE Content_Management_System SHALL focus on luxury homes, villas, apartments, offices, and interior designers as target audience
3. WHEN presenting information, THE Content_Management_System SHALL use short and impactful sentences
4. WHEN describing services, THE Content_Management_System SHALL emphasize trust-building and premium quality
5. WHEN users view calls-to-action, THE Content_Management_System SHALL use luxury-appropriate language like "Get a Free Inspection" and "View Our Work"

### Requirement 4: SEO Optimization and URL Structure

**User Story:** As a digital marketer, I want to maintain and improve SEO performance during the rebrand, so that we don't lose search rankings and can target luxury furniture polishing keywords.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN search engines crawl the site, THE SEO_Manager SHALL optimize for keywords like "Wooden Furniture Polishing in Mumbai" and "Luxury Furniture Polishing Services"
2. WHEN generating URLs, THE SEO_Manager SHALL create clean URL structures reflecting the new brand
3. WHEN pages load, THE SEO_Manager SHALL ensure fast loading times for better search rankings
4. WHEN search engines index content, THE SEO_Manager SHALL provide schema-friendly markup
5. WHEN updating meta descriptions and titles, THE SEO_Manager SHALL incorporate luxury positioning keywords

### Requirement 5: Asset and Media Management

**User Story:** As a brand manager, I want all visual assets to align with the luxury brand identity, so that every touchpoint reinforces the premium positioning.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN users view images, THE Asset_Manager SHALL ensure all photos reflect luxury and premium quality
2. WHEN displaying the logo, THE Asset_Manager SHALL use the new "Luxe Wooden Furniture Polishing" branding
3. WHEN showing before/after galleries, THE Asset_Manager SHALL highlight premium furniture and high-quality results
4. WHEN users see icons and graphics, THE Asset_Manager SHALL use gold accents and luxury styling
5. WHEN loading media files, THE Asset_Manager SHALL optimize for fast loading while maintaining quality

### Requirement 6: Navigation and User Experience

**User Story:** As a website visitor, I want smooth and elegant navigation that matches the luxury brand experience, so that I can easily find information and feel confident about the service quality.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN users scroll through pages, THE Navigation_System SHALL provide smooth scrolling experience
2. WHEN users navigate between sections, THE Navigation_System SHALL maintain consistent luxury design elements
3. WHEN users interact with menus, THE Navigation_System SHALL use subtle animations and gold highlights
4. WHEN users access the site on different devices, THE Navigation_System SHALL provide responsive navigation optimized for each screen size
5. WHEN users look for services, THE Navigation_System SHALL organize content to highlight premium service offerings

### Requirement 7: Call-to-Action and Conversion Optimization

**User Story:** As a business owner, I want the website to effectively convert visitors into customers while maintaining the luxury brand experience, so that the rebrand drives business growth.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN users see primary CTAs, THE Component_Library SHALL display gold buttons with "Get a Free Inspection" text
2. WHEN users see secondary CTAs, THE Component_Library SHALL display outline gold buttons with "View Our Work" text
3. WHEN users interact with booking elements, THE Component_Library SHALL maintain luxury aesthetics throughout the conversion process
4. WHEN users view contact information, THE Component_Library SHALL present it in a premium, trustworthy manner
5. WHEN users complete actions, THE Component_Library SHALL provide elegant confirmation feedback

### Requirement 8: Content Migration and URL Management

**User Story:** As a technical manager, I want to properly migrate all existing content to the new brand while maintaining SEO value, so that we don't lose traffic or search rankings during the transition.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN migrating content, THE Content_Management_System SHALL update all references from "A1 Furniture Polish" to "Luxe Wooden Furniture Polishing"
2. WHEN updating URLs, THE SEO_Manager SHALL implement proper redirects from old URLs to new branded URLs
3. WHEN updating file names, THE Asset_Manager SHALL rename all assets to reflect the new brand naming convention
4. WHEN updating internal links, THE Navigation_System SHALL ensure all links point to the correct new URLs
5. WHEN updating structured data, THE SEO_Manager SHALL modify schema markup to reflect the new business name and branding
